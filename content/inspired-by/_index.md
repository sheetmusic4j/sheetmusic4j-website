## Standing on the Shoulders of Open Standards

Sheetmusic4J didn't start from a blank page. It builds on an existing open format for exchanging sheet music, takes inspiration from an existing open-source renderer for that format on another platform, and relies on other open standards and resources to parse, play back, draw, and test sheet music.
